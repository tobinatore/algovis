Privacy Policy

AlgoVis.io does not collect, log or share your personal information.


What do we define as 'personal information'?

Our definition of personal data is based on the privacy laws and regulations of the EU, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These are widely regarded as the strongest privacy protections in the world. We consider any information about you or your behavior that can be traced back to you as personal data.

This site does not set cookies, displays no ads, does not use analytics and has no contact forms.

What does this mean for you?


It means that you can visit AlgoVis.io without having to worry where your personal information ends up.
  • Due to us not displaying ads and not using analytics, there is no way for advertising companies to profile your behaviour.
  • Because AlgoVis.io does not set cookies, the site can't remember you, your IP-Adress or any other data automatically transmitted by your browser.
  • As an inherently free service, we do not need to share your data with payment providers.

Third party applications


While we do not collect any type of personal data, AlgoVis.io is currently hosted using GitHub pages. This means that GitHub may collect User Personal Information from visitors, including logs of visitor IP addresses, to comply with legal obligations, and to maintain the security and integrity of the Website and the Service.
